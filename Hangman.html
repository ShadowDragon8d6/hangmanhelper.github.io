Hangman Helper

Welcome to the Hangman Helper!

This will allow you to search through all the known words in PH Hangman to find the word that are similar to that word.

The best way to use this is to guess all that vowels before setting the word to narrow down the choices. Then, to set the word click the set word button and copy and paste the word into the box that pops up. After, just click the search for word button. If the word is not in the database, please message ShadowDragon86 as it might be a word that's not in the database.

This Will Not make strategies for you. If you get T_Under____ and it might be thundershock or thunderbolts the logical thing to do will use S because they both have it and it'll tell you which word it is. This is purely up to you.
